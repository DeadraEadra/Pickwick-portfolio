Ingredients

  • 1 tsp honey
  • 3 lemon slices
  • 4 mint leaves

Method

  1. Boil 200ml of water in a kettle
  2. Let the water cool for a second or two when it's boiled. This will reduce a chance of any hard water deposits from tea kettle ending up in your mug or burning yourself with steam.
  3. Put 1-2 teaspoons of tea into your cup
  4. Pour hot water into the cup
  5. Wait 5 minutes
  6. In the meantime prepare the honey and mint and cut the lemon in slices
  7. Add all of the ingredients to the cup
  8. Enjoy while warm!
