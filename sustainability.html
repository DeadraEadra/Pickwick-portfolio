Fair cultivation

Where does Pickwick come from?

Fair cultivation is our top priority, That is why Pickwick obtains its tea leaves from Rainforest Alliance certified tea plantations. This way we know for sure that our tea is made sustainably and the farmer is supported. This yeas the Pickwick Herbal varieties are RA certified and our goal will be achieved in 2022; the entire Pickwick tea range is RA certified!

Recyclable packaging

We're switching to plastic-free!

Sustainability is becoming a huge part of our mission, which is why we designed new packaging for our products. The pouches are fully-recyclable, and their production takes less energy than usual plastic packaging. Our goal is to eliminate all the plastic used to ship our products and switch to its alternatives till the year of 2022.
